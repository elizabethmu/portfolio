Design Process

My design process is a flexible framework that adapts, evolves and responds to user needs and business requirements. Before rolling up my sleeves to design a new feature, I like to ask what our users want to see and what advantages our products can offer. To choreograph the dance of getting users from A to B, I like to iterate based on the findings of usability tests. I test often and test early to get rid of potential roadblocks.
